Polyhex

Game Instructions:

The puzzle is text-based, where each layer is represented as a grid of boxes. Follow these steps:

  • 1. Type the number (from 1 to 12) corresponding to a piece in the box where you want to place it.
  • 2. Click Save and Go to Generator to store your configuration and start generating solutions.

Each number represents a unique piece, and you can rearrange them to find creative solutions!
