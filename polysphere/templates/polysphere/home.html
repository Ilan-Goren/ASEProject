Polysphere

Welcome to the Puzzle game! Here's how you can enjoy playing:

You have a 5x11 board where you need to place colored pieces to solve the puzzle. There are 12 pieces in different colors available in the pieces deck.

Drag pieces from the deck onto the board to place them. To remove a piece from the board, simply click on it.

Click on a piece in the deck to rotate it. Right-click on a piece in the deck to flip it horizontally.

Use the following buttons to manage the game:

• Reset Board: Clears the board to start over.

• Solve Board: Automatically solves the board for you.

• Show All Possible Solutions: Displays all possible solutions to the puzzle.

Enjoy solving the puzzle and exploring different piece arrangements to complete the challenge!
