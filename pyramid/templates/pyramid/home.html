3D Polysphere Pyramid

Game Instructions:

Place Pieces: Use the available controls or keyboard keys to position and orient the pieces in the 3D pyramid.

Keyboard Controls:

  • Arrow Keys: Move the piece in the respective direction (left, right, forward, backward).
  • Shift + Arrow Up: Move the piece up.
  • Shift + Arrow Down: Move the piece down.

Available Controls:

  • Rotate: Rotate the selected piece to change its orientation.
  • Flip: Flip the selected piece along its axis for a mirrored placement.
  • Change Orientation: Adjust the piece to align it differently in the pyramid.
  • Reset: Reset the selected piece to its original position and orientation.
  • Solve: Automatically solve the puzzle either for an empty board or for partial configurations.

Challenge yourself by arranging pieces to complete the pyramid in different configurations!
